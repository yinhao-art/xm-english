Our requirements

The following four points are very important

Skills & Qualifications

A bachelor’s degree and other credentials (diploma or proof of graduation, NBI clearance, and valid ID). Proficiency in speaking the English language (neutral accent)

Technical Equipment

Highly efficient teaching tools (a laptop or desktop with HD camera, a headset with noise-cancelling features, and a stable Internet connection)

Schedule

Availability to teach during peak hours (6:00 p.m. - 8:30 p.m. Mondays to Fridays; 9:00 a.m. - 8:30 p.m. Saturdays and Sundays)

Teach Environment

A quiet, clutter-free, and well-lit space in your home with a plain white background，Or some interesting backgrounds are also possible

FAQ

  • When will the salary be paid?

    Weekly salary，We pay teachers on a weekly basis，Every Tuesday, the salary for last week's Monday to Sunday will be paid

  • What do I have to do as a home-based online English teacher?

    As a our teacher, your main task is to teach Chinese learners English in fun online video lessons. At the end of the lesson, you evaluate the learner's performance based on set guidelines.

  • Do I need to have any teaching experience to qualify?

    Teaching experience is an advantage but not required. we provides free training to equip teachers with the skills and resources to help them succeed on the platform

  • How do teacher level up and earn more money?

    Level up by meeting certain criteria according to the number of successfully conducted lessons, overall teaching performance, training sessions completed, and certifications earned, among other things.
